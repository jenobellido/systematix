GETTING STARTED

HELLO THERE! Welcome to Systematix.co!
Systematix.co is a PAY ON SITE ticket/seat reservation.
We aim to provide our customers the best online cinema/ticketing reservation without having to pay online!

To know more about booking, click the button above with the name "BOOKING A TICKET".
You can simply click any link above that aligns on your information need.

CREATING AN ACCOUNT

1. Click Register Now.


2. Fill-out required information (Name, Phone Number, Address, Birthdate).


3. Click Next button.
4. Fill-out required information (Email).
5. Enter desired password.
6. Re-enter password to confirm.
6.1 You can click back to edit information (Name, Phone Number, Address, Birthdate) .
7. Click Register Now button.

8. Account Registered.

LOGGING IN TO YOUR ACCOUNT

1. Enter needed information. (Email, Password)


2. Click Login Now

3. Logged in to the website.

VIEWING PROFILE

1. Click "MY PROFILE" on the navigation panel at the top.


2. You'll see your information and the tickets you booked.



CHANGING ACCOUNT INFORMATION

1. Click "MY PROFILE" on the navigation panel at the top.


2. Click the "Edit Profile" button on lower right.


3. Enter new information.


3. Click Confirm and the information is updated.

SIGNING OUT

1. Mouse over or click the logout logo on the lower right of the screen.


2. Click the Log Out button and you'll be logged out and be redirected to login page.

BOOKING A TICKET

1. Click any book now button.


2. Choose Movie ticket to reserve.


3. Click Next button.
4. Review information that was automatically stated.


5. Choose Schedule (date must be minimum of 3 days after the current date of booking)
6. Choose Time.
7. Choose Cinema.
8. Click Next Button. (Click back to go back to movie selection.)


9. Click Submit Button. (Click back to go back to information review.)
10. Booking requested.

The booking is pending. The staff will review your booking request in minimum of 24 hours.

CONTACTING US

1. Click "ABOUT" on the navigation panel at the top.


2. Click "CONTACT US" button to view different platforms to reach us.

3. Click "ABOUT US" button to go back to "ABOUT" page.

CINEMAS MOVIE LIST

1. Click "CINEMAS" on the navigation panel at the top.


2. Click "View What's Showing" on any cinema to view available movies on that branch.

LIGHT/DARK THEME

1. Click light/dark button on the lower right of the screen based on your preference.
